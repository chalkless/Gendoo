Association data

  • score.[gene|omim].[type].[category].tab
    • *.A.tab: Anatomy
    • *.B.tab: Organisms
    • *.C.tab: Diseases
    • *.D.tab: Drugs and chemicals
    • *.G.tab: Biological phenomena
    • *.S.tab: Substance Names

    Association files are tab-separated text data containing Gene/OMIM ID, score (information gain), p-value and MeSH ID.

  • Gene names and aliases (2009/1/8 ver.)
  • Disease names and aliases (2009/1/8 ver.)
